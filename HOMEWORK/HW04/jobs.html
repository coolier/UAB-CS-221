Barista

A barista is a coffee expert responsible for preparing and serving a variety of coffee and tea beverages while providing exceptional customer service. Starting pay is $12 an hour plus tips!

  • Handling Transactions
  • Maintaining Cleanliness
  • Ensuring a Positive Customer Experience

DJ

A DJ is responsible for selecting and playing music at JavaJam. You will use our specialized equipment to mix and transition between songs, keeping the audience entertained and engaged. Our DJs often work with event organizers to create a memorable musical experience. Starting pay is $15 an hour!

  • Music Selection
  • Mixing and Blending
  • Equiment Setup/Maintenance
  • Announcements
